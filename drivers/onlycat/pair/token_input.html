How to find your device token:

  1. Open the OnlyCat app on your phone
  2. Go to the "Account" section
  3. Look for "Device Token" or "API Token"
  4. Copy the token (it's a long string of letters and numbers)
  5. Paste it in the field below

Note: If you can't find the token in the app, please contact OnlyCat support.
